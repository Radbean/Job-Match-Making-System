## 1. How does effective communication reduce project risks?

Effective communication reduces risks by:
- Identifying issues early before they escalate
- Ensuring everyone understands goals and expectations
- Facilitating quick decision-making
- Building trust and preventing misunderstandings
- Keeping stakeholders informed and engaged
